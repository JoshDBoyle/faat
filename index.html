Lynn Alexander

Lynn B. Alexander is a retired Air Force Colonel and General Electric Aircraft Engines retiree. He received his Masters Degree in Industrial Management from Central Michigan University. After twenty-four years of several leadership roles with the Air Force, he retired and entered the commercial world with General Electric Aircraft Engines. His experience ranges from Configuration Management of Space vehicles, instructor pilot, production, Air Force leader of Program Depot Level Maintenance of Presidential aircraft, Procurement and Director of Contracting.

In civilian life, he was manager of Sourcing Administration, Aircraft engine supplies and equipment procurement and engine spare parts customer service. After retirement, Lynn became very active as an instructor in gliders.

John Conover

John Conover is a former U.S. Marine who obtained the rank of Captain while serving in Aviation supply and as an Infantry Officer. John received his Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration from Morehead State University in Morehead, KY. He was a 30 year employee of GE where his specialties were quality, compliance and auditing.

Positions held at GE included:

  • Manager of Quality Assurance and Auditing
  • Policy Compliance Manager
  • Senior Product Quality Engineer

John also received the President’s Award and GE Management Award from GE Aircraft.

John’s areas of expertise include:

  • ISO Standard 17025/ANSI/Z540
  • ISO 13485/ISO 9001
  • ASQ-Certified Quality Auditor-2007
  • FAA Orders and Directives

Currently, John is an Audit and Quality/Manufacturing Systems consultant and Quality Auditor
